Football is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each. At the beginning of the 21st century, it was played by over 250 million players in over 200 countries, making it the most popular sport in the world.

It is played with a spherical ball on a rectangular field, covered with grass, with a goal at each end. The object of the game is to score goals by putting the ball into the opponent's goal.

In the spring of 2001 , FIFA reported that more than 240 million people were playing football regularly in more than 200 countries around the world. Its simple rules and the low and cheap equipment needed to play football have undoubtedly contributed to its growing popularity. In many parts of the world, football gives birth to enormous passions and plays a very important role in the lives of fans, local communities and even nations; It is often called the most popular sport in the world.
